Special Thanks

We would like to make a special note towards the inspiration for this project - Musical Pairs. The inspiration for this project was given after coming across a psychology experiment carried out by a UCC Masters student - Pietro Rizzo. We later got in contact with Pietro and asked him if he would be okay with us recreating and expanding on his idea. Pietro was more than obliging with us and providing us with all the information we needed to recreate the project, as well as given us further input on what he would've liked to have seen. We appreciate your time and enthusiasm shown to us.
To further acknowledge his support, we would like to ask people to help out with his research by checking out his Musical Pairs experiment, the link of which is displayed below. Thank you.
